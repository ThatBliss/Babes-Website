Join Babes

We are always looking for dedicated and friendly players to join our ranks. Whether you are a seasoned raider or a new player, we have a place for you in our community.

Join us for epic adventures, personal growth, and a guild that feels like home!

Who We Are

  • Established Guild: Babes has been around since 2023.
  • Invested in Our Players: We offer log reviews and tools to help you improve at your own pace, with zero pressure.
  • Socially Focused: We believe the guild experience is about more than just gameplay—it’s about the people. We prioritize creating a positive, social environment where members fit with our relaxed, fun-loving mentality.
  • Supportive Community: A welcoming and inclusive environment.
  • Non-Toxic and Inclusive: No raging, no drama. We’re all here to have fun and grow together.
  • Patience: We are a guild that values patience and understanding.
  • Our Goal: Each raid tier, our goal is to clear all content on heoric, after that we try to clear as much mythic as we are able to.

What We Offer

  • Regular Raids: Fridays from 20:00 to 23:00 server time.
  • Mythic+ Dungeons: Groups running most nights.
  • Supportive Community: A welcoming and inclusive environment.
  • Discord: Active voice and text channels for communication and fun.

What We Expect

  • The Social Aspect: We pride us in putting the Social Aspect above all else, so we obviously expect the same from our members.
  • Respect: Respect for your fellow guildies and the community.
  • Communication: We expect you to communicate with us if you can't make it to a raid or if you have any issues.
  • Preparation: We expect you to come prepared to raids, with consumables and ready for banter.
  • Patience: We expect you to be patient with us and with yourself.
