Recommended Addons

!UNDER DEVOLPMENT!

Here we will post various addons and weakauras we recommend our guildies to use:

  • Deadly Boss Mods (DBM): Provides alerts and timers for boss abilities.
  • WeakAuras: Allows you to display highly customizable graphics on your screen to indicate buffs, debuffs, and other important information.
  • Details! Damage Meter: A comprehensive damage meter that tracks your performance in raids and dungeons.
  • GTFO: Alerts you when you are standing in harmful effects.
  • ElvUI: A complete UI overhaul that replaces the default Blizzard UI with a more modern and customizable interface.
